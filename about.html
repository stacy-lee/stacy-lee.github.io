My Story

On the Path to Data Science

I enjoy learning new algorithms and programming languages. Alongside the requirements of my undergraduate major and minor, I managed to take several computer science courses such as discrete structures and data structures. I minored in mathematical statistics because I really wanted to take a deeper dive into theoretical statistics to understand how to apply it better. Some time near the completion of my undergraduate requirements, I realized that I liked my minor more than my major, so I decided to pursue a master's degree in statistics.

During my free time, I like to play volleyball and catch up on TV shows. I also want to play more tennis and badminton.
